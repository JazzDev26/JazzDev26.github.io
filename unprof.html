FAQs

What is UNITE 2030?

UNITE 2030 is a part of the United Nations. UNITE 2030's mission is to empower the next generation of young leaders to solve the biggest challenges that the world faces - the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals. (SDGs) UNITE 2030’s organisational values guide everything that we do, both internally and externally. We expect that our community members, Chapter Presidents, and volunteers live in alignment
with our core values during their work with the organisation and outside.
Our values are: Action, Leadership, Generosity, Innovation, Bravery, and Unity.

What is YDP 6?

The Youth Delegate Program (YDP) is a 3-month intensive leadership development program for the world’s top young Changemakers who are taking action to achieve the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals by 2030.
